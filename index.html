About Me

As a recent Magna Cum Laude graduate in Information Technology from Centro Escolar University - Makati, I have specialized in network security and software development, bringing strong analytical skills in database management and system administration. My commitment to continuous learning and the adoption of emerging technologies drives me to enhance organizational efficiency and success. Throughout my academic career, I have been recognized with notable achievements including the Leadership Award and being a Dean’s Lister. My roles as President of the CSIT Student Council and Auditor of JPCS showcase my leadership abilities and transparent management skills.

In addition to my academic accomplishments, I have contributed to various projects such as the development of the Branch Information and Incident Report for Network Strengthening (BIIRDNEST) System and an E-Commerce Website using Java and Spring Boot. My technical expertise spans several programming languages and frameworks including Java, Python, Spring Boot, and Django. My work experience includes an internship at Philippine Savings Bank where I improved operational efficiency through an Inventory System and an OJT role at First Eduspec Inc. focusing on robotics research. I hold certifications in cybersecurity from Fortinet and NC II in Computer Systems Servicing from TESDA, complementing my skills in leadership, project management, and technical problem-solving.
